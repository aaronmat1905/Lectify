FAQs

What is Lectify?

Lectify is an AI-powered EdTech platform that transforms educational material into personalized courses. It also generates course content in Indian languages like Hindi, Kannada, Malayalam, Tamil, and Telugu, with support for additional languages available on demand.

Where can I use Lectify?

Lectify is currently in active development and testing. However, we offer a service to generate educational videos based on your requirements. Once processed, we can send the materials directly to you. If you're interested, fill out this Google Form to get started.

How does Lectify Work?

Lectify uses AI to analyze educational content and structure it into customized courses. It can generate learning material, including videos, summaries, and quizzes, making education more accessible and engaging.

Who can benefit from Lectify?

Lectify is designed for students, educators, and institutions looking for AI-generated, structured learning content. Whether you're an independent learner or part of an educational institution, Lectify can help simplify complex subjects.
